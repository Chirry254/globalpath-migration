Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GlobalPath and your migration journey.

💳 Payment
How much does the assessment cost? ▼
The migration eligibility assessment costs $20 USD. Payment is processed securely through Stripe and you can pay with Visa, Mastercard or PayPal.
Is my payment secure? ▼
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, one of the world's most trusted payment platforms. Your card details are never stored on our servers.
Can I get a refund? ▼
Assessment fees are non-refundable once the exam has been started. If you experience a payment issue without accessing the exam, contact us within 48 hours for a full refund.
Does PayPal work for payment? ▼
Yes! PayPal is accepted through our Stripe checkout. You will see the PayPal option on the payment page after clicking Pay Now.
🌎 Country Eligibility
Which countries does GlobalPath cover? ▼
We currently cover 10 top migration destinations: USA, Canada, UK, Australia, Germany, New Zealand, Netherlands, France, UAE and Japan. More countries are being added.
How do I know which country is right for me? ▼
Our assessment test evaluates your profile against each country's immigration requirements. You can also browse each country's page to compare requirements, visa types, and costs before taking the test.
What factors affect my eligibility? ▼
Key factors include your education level, work experience, language proficiency, age, financial status, and whether you have family connections in the destination country.
Can I take the assessment for multiple countries? ▼
Each assessment fee covers one country. If you would like to assess eligibility for multiple destinations, each requires a separate $20 payment.
📝 Assessment Exam
How long does the assessment take? ▼
The assessment consists of 20 questions and typically takes 10 to 15 minutes to complete. Take your time ‚Äî there is no time limit.
What happens after I complete the assessment? ▼
You will receive an instant eligibility score and a detailed report showing your strengths, areas to improve, and recommended next steps for your chosen destination.
Can I retake the assessment? ▼
Yes, but each attempt requires a new $20 payment. We recommend reviewing your results and improving your profile before retaking.
Is the assessment result a visa guarantee? ▼
No. The assessment gives you an eligibility estimate based on current immigration criteria. Final visa decisions are made by the immigration authorities of each country. We recommend consulting a licensed immigration consultant for official applications.
